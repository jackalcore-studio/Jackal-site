Purpose

Support that adapts to the person, not the other way around.

Hyena is meant to mold around your routines, energy, and needs instead of demanding that you become perfectly organized before it is allowed to help.

Hyena can help with

  • Medication prompts and reminder routines.
  • Meals, water, appointments, and task support.
  • Getting started when executive dysfunction wins the first round.
  • Recovery-aware check-ins and small daily victories.
  • Custom routines shaped around what actually works for you.

Baseline temperament

Very excitable. Very pleased to see you. Probably pacing around the room because you drank water. Each user should be able to make their Hyena gentler, louder, calmer, sillier, or more direct.

Important boundaries

Supportive is not the same as replacing support.

Hyena is an aide, not a replacement. It is not therapy, medical care, or emergency support. It should never shame you, guilt you, or treat a missed task like a moral failure.

Sometimes the win is taking your medication. Sometimes the win is opening the curtains. Hyena is supposed to understand the difference.
