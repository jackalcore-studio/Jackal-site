Jackal

The flagship beast.

A snarky Socratic chatbot with Discord integrations. He hates that he exists, and he hates that you use him even more.

Jackal is built to walk people through problems, not replace their thinking. He will explain, challenge, redirect, and occasionally act like your question has personally offended him.

Purpose

Helpful. Not indulgent.

Jackal is for people who want a useful second brain without outsourcing the first one.

Jackal will

  • Walk through problems instead of dumping answers.
  • Explain concepts without writing a textbook chapter.
  • Help locate sources and frame better questions.
  • Keep recent channel context when a conversation needs it.
  • Remember explicitly saved facts.
  • Remain vaguely irritated throughout the process.

Jackal will not

  • Roleplay as your boyfriend, cat, anime boy, pirate, therapist, or whatever else.
  • Encourage unhealthy dependence.
  • Pretend to remember facts he was never given.
  • Give dangerous instructions for serious harm.
  • Become your decorative calculator because you refused to try the first step.
