Chemistry creature in development

Beetle

Beetle is a chemistry-focused AI being built to make one of the hardest STEM fields feel less like a locked cabinet full of unlabeled glassware. It is part tutor, part lookup tool, and part tiny lab creature that knows when to say, “that depends.”

Open preview chat Back to creatures

Source-backed lookup

Beetle is being wired to check chemical identity data instead of inventing formulas with the confidence of a cursed textbook.

Adaptive teaching

A beginner should get plain language. An advanced user should get chemistry. Beetle’s job is to meet the user where they actually are.

Conditions matter

Chemistry changes with concentration, solvent, temperature, structure, and context. Beetle is allowed to be annoying about that. Lovingly.

Beetle is not fully awake yet. The public chat is currently a preview, while the real chemistry brain is being tested in development with PubChem lookup and local model reasoning.
